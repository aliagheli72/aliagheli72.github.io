Privacy Policy.

How NeuroTech Frontiers collects, uses, and protects your information.

This is a template policy provided with the website build and should be reviewed by legal counsel before launch. Last updated: June 2026.

Information we collect

When you join our waitlist, apply to speak, or submit a sponsorship inquiry, we collect the information you provide — such as your name, email address, and organization. We also collect limited, anonymized analytics about how visitors use the site.

How we use your information

We use your information to respond to your inquiry, send you conference updates you have requested, process registrations, and improve the event experience. We do not sell your personal data.

Sharing

We share information only with service providers that help us operate the conference (for example, email and database providers), and only as needed to deliver the services you requested.

Data retention

We retain submissions for as long as necessary to operate the event and our communications with you, after which they are deleted or anonymized.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us. You can unsubscribe from updates using the link in any email.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us through the contact form on our homepage and we’ll respond promptly.
